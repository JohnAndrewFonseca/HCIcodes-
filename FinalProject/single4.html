Tools

A surprisingly big improvement may be achieved when creating and customising your keyboard with the proper tools.

Essential Mechanical Keyboard Tools:

Keycap puller and Switch puller
> it is design to remove the keycaps and switch from your keyboard with ease and without damaging your keyboard. You can buy a 2in1 puller which has both keycap and switch puller.

Switch Opener
> In addition to saving time, the switch opener facilitates the procedure and reduces the risk of breaking nails or injuring fingertips. Additionally, it guards against the potential scraping and scratching that can happen to switches if you try to open them with your nail or another instrument.

Precision Screwdriver Set
> screws are usually rather tiny, accessing and removing them calls for a set of precision screwdrivers. If you don't have a switch opener, you can still open switches with precision screwdrivers.

Stem Holder
> A stem holder is a small tool used to secure switches while lubricating them. This is critical as switches may be challenging to operate. They are readily dropped, and while lubricating or shooting, they may be subjected to excessive pressure.

Small brush
> a small brush or thin brush is used to lube the switch and stabilizer.
